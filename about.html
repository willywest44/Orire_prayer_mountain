About Us

ORIRE PRAYER MOUNTAIN was established November 2011, situated at Afeki Opa, off Ife-Ilesha Road, Ile-Ife,Osun state occupies land expanse of about four hectares. It is an upspring of HIS JEWEL MINISTRIES with a clear mandate of catering for those seeking solutions to life complex situation, those going through crisis and christians facing life battles. Salvation of souls (being born again) through confession and forsaken of sinful ways and accepting Jesus Christ as personal Lord and Saviour forms the basis of the mountain's covenant of getting life problems solved by faith through prayer and fasting. God through the years have manifested His ultimate power in delivering the oppressed,healing the sick,giving children to those seeking the fruit of the womb and receiving financial breakthrough/prosperity. We believe nothing is impossible with the God of covenant of mercy and goodness at ORIRE PRAYER MOUNTAIN and so encourage men from all works of life to avail themselves this glorious opportunity of fulfilling life and ministrial destiny through prayers while keeping eternity in view regardless of their religious beliefs or denomination. You are cordially welcome !
